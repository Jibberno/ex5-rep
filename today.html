The Smiling Man

Feb 28, 2023

Camping in the middle of Appalachia, I noticed a man from the distance. A man that completely seemed out of place for an environment like this. He was well dressed, and looked like he took care of himself. What set me off was noticing his big smile that spread across his face. This wasn't no ordinary smile, this smile looked like it would hurt the average person if they were to try and do such a thing. But this man was doing it for minutes without a look of stress in his eyes.

But I couldn't help but be at ease.
